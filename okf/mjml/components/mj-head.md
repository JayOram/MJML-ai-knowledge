# mj-head

Holds head-level components. It has no attributes of its own. Children include [mj-attributes](/components/mj-attributes.md), [mj-style](/components/mj-style.md), [mj-font](/components/mj-font.md), [mj-include](/components/mj-include.md), `mj-title`, `mj-preview`, `mj-breakpoint`, and `mj-html-attributes`.

Defining defaults here keeps templates DRY — see [mj-attributes](/components/mj-attributes.md) for the attribute cascade.
